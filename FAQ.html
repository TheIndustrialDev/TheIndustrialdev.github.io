Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the questions I have been frequently asked by the people who have hired me to write storylines for them.

Q: "I do not have robux I can pay you with directly. Is it ok if I pay you with percentage profits instead?"

A: If you do not have applicable funds to pay me with directly, we are able to discuss potential other means of payment. However, if this is the case, your story will be set as a "second-priority" writing project. In other words, those who pay directly with robux have a higher priority with their stories than those with percentages, and will likely get theirs done first. There are exceptions to this, of course, but for the majority of the time, this will be the case.

---

Q: "Are getting the Extra Products required?"

A: No product is required to get the others. One could just as easily buy a story without a map as someone could just buy a timeline without any storyline altogether. It's all up to the customer.

---

Q: "What if there is something I don't like about the storyline?"

A: If you wish with further assistance with your storyline after it has already been written by me, contact me and we can work something out. Most of the time, storyline edits will be free of charge.

---

Q: "You have all of these stories in your Past Projects. Which packages do they each go with?"

A: None of those projects were made during the time I had the packages set up; however, if I were to set them with a package, this is probably how I would set them up (keep in mind - my writing has enhanced since these stories were written, I just dont have any current examples with fully released games to show);

Scribe Pack: The Factory, WSP-1, Rhythm Stars: Duncan Vissen

Author Pack: The Osten Empire, The Konomi Knights, Robot 64

Novelist Pack: None of the currently written stories are Novelist Pack. Just imagine the Author Pack stories, but on major overload mode.

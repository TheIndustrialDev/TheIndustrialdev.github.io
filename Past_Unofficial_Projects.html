Past Unofficial Projects

These are the past projects that have NOT either been officially turned into games or are officially going to be a future game.

The Factory

Finished October 27th, 2017

Written for Roblox developer lavafactory for a first project, The Factory is a small story revolving around, you guessed it, a factory. The story follows Detective Patrick Undale, a junior detective bound to the mysterious case revolving around the Marcus Westfield Factory, where his partner died attempting to solve the same case.

Read the story here

The Osten Empire

Finished October 29th, 2017

Written for Roblox developer RBLX_Dan, this story is set within the Medieval world of Haus. With entire kingdoms being ridden to dust by the mighty Osten Empire and the entire world soon following, a young soldier by the name of Joseph Harp must learn his strengths and weaknesses to fight off the most dangerous superpower the world has ever seen.

Read the story here

The Komori Knights

Finished November 1st, 2017

Written for Universia_IV, this story is set within the (also medieval) world of Marthis. Following the tale of the Komori Knights, guardians of the vault containing the most evil relics of the Middle Ages world, knight Tamashi must make strange alliances, both with new knights and ancient beings, to guide her way towards defeating Marsha, the darkest warrior to ever threaten the world onto the brink of doomsday.

Read the story here

WSP-1

Finished November 11th, 2017

Another story written for Roblox developer lavafactory, this story revolves around scientist Daniel Mineza, living in the distant future on the water planet WSP-1. With a base overridden by a dangerous disease and the fate of all mankind in their hands, Daniel must make a startling decision in an attempt to save humanity from extinction.
